# Identity Protection

**Series:** Identity Protection  
**Posts:** 1 published · more coming  
**Exam alignment:** SC-300 · AZ-500

---

Entra ID Protection is Entra's built-in risk engine. It scores every sign-in and every user based on signals — leaked credentials, impossible travel, anonymous IPs, and dozens of others. But the default setup has gaps that cause real-world incidents: false positives that lock out legitimate users, risk states that never remediate, and policies that fire when you don't expect them to.

This series covers the operational patterns that docs skip.

---

## Before starting this series
